How to make toast water at home

Toast Water is a deluctable and refreshing beverage that has been enjoyed for generations. It is easy to prepare and a pleasure to enjoy. Come make it with us!


Ingredients

  • Toast
  • Boiling water

Directions

  1. Break toast in pieces, add to preferred drinking cup and add water and allow to stand 1 hour.

  2. Strain through cheesecloth, season, and serve hot or cold.

  3. Enjoy the next 24 hours.
